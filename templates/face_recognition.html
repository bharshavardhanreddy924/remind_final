How It Works

The face recognition system helps you identify people in photos by comparing them with reference faces you've added.

Steps to use:
  1. First, add reference faces of people you want to recognize
  2. Upload a photo containing people's faces
  3. The system will identify faces it recognizes
  4. View results showing names and confidence levels
Tips:
  • Use clear, well-lit photos for best results
  • Front-facing portraits work better than side profiles
  • Add multiple reference photos of the same person for better accuracy
